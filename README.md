# Ghost of Kyiv
Ghost of Kyiv Browser Game.

Play as the Famous Ghost of Kyiv Hero of the russio-Ukrainian war of 2023.

Burn russian scum, armor, shoot down planes!

And of course, Burn down the Kremlin.
